Effects on Student Exam Performance

Exploring the various variables than can influence American High-Schooler's exam grades

In this interactive slideshow you will be presented data about the variables than can impact the test scores of high schoolers and will have the ability to look at the data in a variety of different ways.

The primary questions this slideshow tries to answer are: "What variables effect student performance the most" and "How much of the varience between different groups of highschoolers can be explained by whether or not they prepared for the exam?"

Disclaimer: This dataset was generated and is being analysed for educational purposes, do not use this data or this analysis to draw conclusions about the real world.

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/spscientist/students-performance-in-exams/data
